Crystal Booksters was founded in March of 2019, by two aspiring coders: Kene Ochuba and Arun Atchuthananthan. The site was inspired by both of the founders love of reading. They want to create a welcoming environment where booksters around the world can discuss their reads and imagine outcomes with other booksters!
